Qingfeng Lan

〖 CV / Google Scholar / Github / LinkedIn / Twitter 〗

I’m a final year PhD Student at the University of Alberta, advised by Prof. A. Rupam Mahmood.
My current research focuses reinforcement learning, continual learning, embodied AI, LLM with human feedback, and meta learning.

Generally, I’m interested in developing simple and efficient learning algorithms supported by sound theories and verified by rigorous experiments. I believe that the ability to extract, accumulate, and exploit knowledge continually and efficiently is essential for the success of artificial general intelligence in the real world.

In particular, my PhD research focuses on designing (continual) reinforcement learning algorithms with higher sample, memory, and computation efficiency. I have worked on meta learning, exploration, language modelling, and quantum reinforcement learning. Recently, I’m also intereseted in embodied AI and large language models with human feedback.

I’m on the job market and actively looking for a Research Scientist position starting in 2025!
